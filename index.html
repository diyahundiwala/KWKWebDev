Our mission is to iniSHEate change within the tech field and inspire young women and nonbinary people to pursue careers in technology. This website is our Kode With Klossy (KWK) final project. After a KWK Culture in Tech session, we were all feeling pretty inspired, motivated, and shocked by the lack of diversity within the field of technology. Plenty of young women and non-binary people are interested in the tech field. However, based on our personal experience, we know that the number of resources available can be scarce or often overwhelming. Through iniSHEate, we want to streamline valuable and reliable resources for young women and nonbinary people, and provide them with a platform to get educated and inspired!
